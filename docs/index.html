What is Tokémon

Tokémon is a configurable token metrics insight and trading platform. It enables viewing the latest token metrics (token and trading stats) through a powerful realtime dashboard. You can track tokens by various on-chain metrics (see insights for more info).

Tokémon also enables trading services on decentralized exchanges (such as UniSwap) through a powerful bot, whose main goal is to maximize your profits by using advanced trading strategies. The configuration of our tools are very modular - you can make use of pseudo limit order buy, sell, trailing-buy and trailing-sell strategies as you would on a convetional centralised exchange

Those are just some of our features. We're in the process of developing and brainstorming more features (such as new tools and extending the current ones). Our plans are to align our thoughts with community's requirements in order to make the platform as good as it can be.

How it works

Our tools provide a valuable data to you, by scanning and analyzing the underlying data sources in real time and summarizing the data. For example for our insights and trading tool we scan the Ethereum blockchain in real time, parse the block data and look for useful insights. For each block we summarize and store the useful information, match it with your configurations and notify you with insights/trading strategies.

Trades and trading strategies are executed by the trading bot client.

How to use the platform

The only requirement for using the platform is holding certain amount of Tokemon tokens. The exact amount is dependent on the plan that you want to use. You can increase/decrease your token holdings for upgrading/downgrading your plan. If you're not satisfied with the product, you can always trade the tokens on the Uniswap exchange. Currently there are no financial initiatives for holding the utility token (other than making profit if the token value increases), but there might be in the future.

We provide insights into the latest trading metrics from Uniswap and other DEXes. Make profit by letting the automated trading platform work for you.
