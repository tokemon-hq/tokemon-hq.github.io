General FAQ

  • Will future updates be included in my current subscription?

    Yes, all the future updates will be added to the current plans. However some feature will be added to some of the plans (E.g. trading related features will be added to the trading related plans). You can always upgrade or downgrade your plan by holding the appropriate amount of tokens.

  • How do you decide on implementing new features?

    We're looking at what's most useful and necessary, but we also look at what our community desires the most.

  • What are the unique features of this platform?

    Our mission is to offer the most accurate and easy to use trading insight platform, that will assist you in making profits and also help you make better trading decisions. The platform is designed to be modular, flexible and focused on the end user. This allows for various different configurations and almost unlimited possibilities for new features and use cases.

  • Will there be other exchanges supported?

    Yes, we're going to support other DEXes too. Our plan is to support Sushiswap as a second exchange. If you feel that some other Exchange should be supported, don't hesitate to suggest it.

  • What can we expect in the future?

    We have created a list of features which are on our TO-DO list, but this is not the end. We're constantly working on improving the product and adding new features. Our goal is to always have something on the feature list, prioritizing the things which are most wanted by our community.

  • What is the idea behind the tokenized subscriptions?

    The main idea is that this is the most blockchain native way for a subscription model. The tokenized subscription model gives more flexibility to you as a user and also works in other ways than just a subscription. Currently the token only serves as a utility, but in the future other benefits might be available for the token holders. The tokenized subscription model also makes the development team more accountable as the token value very closely represents the success of the project.
